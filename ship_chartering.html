Ship Chartering & brokering

Our company specializes in providing comprehensive ship chartering services for all types of cargo and catering. Caspian International Services unit is dedicated to offering a complete suite of supply services to both local and international ships operating within the Caspian Sea region. Our chartering department, led by a highly experienced team with a wealth of expertise in the field of chartering, works closely with all relevant parties. This includes ship-owners, charterers, operators, trading houses, exporters, and importers of various types of cargoes. Our primary objective is to ensure that their chartering needs are met promptly and in accordance with their specific laycan requirements. We understand the importance of efficient and reliable chartering services, and we strive to facilitate smooth operations and meet the unique demands of our clients
